This guide covers everything candidates need to know before taking a proctored test on AutoProctor. Read through these instructions carefully to avoid losing your answers.

<Warning>
For non-Socratease quizzes, you must click the submit buttons in the correct order. If you skip the purple button, **all your answers will be lost**.
</Warning>

## Try the Demo Test First

AutoProctor requires modern browser technologies to function. Before your actual test, try the demo to confirm your device works:

[Take the Demo Test](https://www.autoproctor.co/tests/start-test/GFICZZZA)

If the demo loads successfully, your assigned test should work too.

## Submit Buttons

For non-Socratease quizzes (Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, etc.), there are **two submit buttons** that must be clicked in the correct order:

1. **Purple Submit Button** (bottom) — click this **first** to save your answers
2. **Green Submit Button** (top) — click this **second** to submit the proctored session

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/taking-tests/submit-buttons-proctored.png" alt="Purple and green submit buttons showing the correct click order" />
</Frame>

<Note>
Socratease quizzes have only **one submit button**, so there is no risk of answer loss. See [Submit Button Differences](/taking-tests/submit-button) for details.
</Note>

## Google Account

If you are taking a Google Forms test:

- Log out of **all** Google accounts before starting the test
- Log in with the correct account when prompted
- **Do not** use Incognito or Private browsing mode

## Browser and Device Requirements

| Device | Browser | Minimum Version |
|---|---|---|
| iPhone / iPad | Safari | 13+ |
| Windows / Android / Mac | Google Chrome | 83+ |

- Your device should ideally be less than 3 years old
- AutoProctor runs AI algorithms on your camera and audio feeds, which requires adequate processing power
