<Warning>
Read this page carefully, especially the section about submit buttons. If you don't follow the correct submission order, **all your answers will be lost**.
</Warning>

## Try the Demo Test First

AutoProctor requires modern browser technologies to function. Before your actual test, try the demo to confirm your device works:

[Take the Demo Test](https://www.autoproctor.co/tests/start-test/GFICZZZA)

If the demo loads successfully, your assigned test should work too.

## Submit Buttons

For non-Socratease quizzes (Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, etc.), there are **two submit buttons**:

1. **Purple Submit Button** (bottom) — click this **first** to save your answers
2. **Green Submit Button** (top) — click this **second** to submit the proctored session

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/taking-tests/submit-buttons-proctored.png" alt="Purple and green submit buttons" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
If you don't click the **purple button first**, all the answers you entered will be lost.
</Warning>

## Google Account

- Log out of **all** Google accounts before starting the test
- Log in with the correct account when prompted
- **Do not** use Incognito or Private browsing mode

## Browser and Device Requirements

| Device | Browser | Minimum Version |
|---|---|---|
| iPhone / iPad | Safari | 13+ |
| Windows / Android / Mac | Google Chrome | 83+ |

- Your device should ideally be less than 3 years old
- AutoProctor runs AI algorithms on your camera and audio feeds, which requires significant processing power
