---
title: "How to Logout of AutoProctor"
description: "Steps to log out of your AutoProctor session on mobile and desktop devices so you can switch to a different account."
---

You may need to log out of AutoProctor if you want to switch to a different account or if you accidentally logged in with the wrong credentials. The logout process differs slightly between mobile and desktop devices.

## On Mobile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the menu">
    Tap the **three horizontal bars** (menu icon) at the top left of your screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Logout">
    Tap **Logout** from the menu options.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## On Desktop

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click on your name">
    Click on your **name** at the top right of the screen (where it says "Hi ...").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Logout">
    Click **Logout** from the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Alternative Method

If the standard logout does not work, you can force a logout by signing out of all your Google accounts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Google's logout page">
    Go to [accounts.google.com/Logout](https://accounts.google.com/Logout) to sign out of all your Google accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log back in to AutoProctor">
    Navigate back to your test link and sign in with the correct account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
