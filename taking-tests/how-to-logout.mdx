---
title: "How to Logout of AutoProctor"
description: "Steps to log out of your AutoProctor session on mobile and desktop."
---

You need to log out if you want to access AutoProctor with a different account.

## On Mobile

Click on the **three horizontal bars** at the top left of your screen, then click **Logout**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/taking-tests/logout-mobile.jpeg" alt="Logout on mobile" />
</Frame>

## On Desktop

Click on your **name** at the top right where it says "Hi ..." and then click **Logout**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/taking-tests/logout-desktop.png" alt="Logout on desktop" />
</Frame>

## Alternative Method

If the standard logout doesn't work, log out of all your Google Accounts by visiting [accounts.google.com/Logout](https://accounts.google.com/Logout), then log back in to AutoProctor.
