---
title: "How to Logout of AutoProctor"
description: "Steps to log out of your AutoProctor session on mobile and desktop devices."
---

You may need to log out of AutoProctor if you want to switch to a different account or if you accidentally logged in with the wrong credentials.

## On Mobile

Tap the **three horizontal bars** (menu icon) at the top left of your screen, then tap **Logout**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/taking-tests/logout-mobile.jpeg" alt="Logout option in the AutoProctor mobile menu" />
</Frame>

## On Desktop

Click on your **name** at the top right (where it says "Hi ..."), then click **Logout**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/taking-tests/logout-desktop.png" alt="Logout option in the AutoProctor desktop header" />
</Frame>

## Alternative Method

If the standard logout does not work, log out of all your Google Accounts by visiting [accounts.google.com/Logout](https://accounts.google.com/Logout), then log back in to AutoProctor with the correct account.
