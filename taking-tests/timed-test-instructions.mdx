This guide covers everything candidates need to know before taking a timed test on AutoProctor. Even though proctoring is not active, the timer and submit button rules still apply.

## Try the Demo Test First

Before your actual test, confirm your setup by trying the demo:

[Take the Demo Test](https://www.autoproctor.co/tests/start-test/cQAtQHp7Jz)

## Submit Buttons

For non-Socratease quizzes, there are **two submit buttons** that must be clicked in the correct order:

1. **Purple Submit Button** (bottom) — click this **first** to save your answers
2. **Green Submit Button** (top) — click this **second** to submit the timed session

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/taking-tests/submit-buttons-timed.gif" alt="Submit button sequence for timed tests" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
If you do not click the **purple button first**, all your answers will be lost.
</Warning>

<Note>
Socratease quizzes have only **one submit button**, so there is no risk of answer loss. See [Submit Button Differences](/taking-tests/submit-button) for details.
</Note>

## Google Account

If you are taking a Google Forms test:

- Log out of all Google accounts at [accounts.google.com/Logout](https://accounts.google.com/Logout) before starting
- Log in with the correct account when prompted
- **Do not** use Incognito or Private browsing mode
