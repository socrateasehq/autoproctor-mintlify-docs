Every AutoProctor test begins with an instructions page that explains how proctoring works, which features are active, and what candidates should expect. The page content adapts automatically based on your test configuration, and candidates can also take a demo test from this page to verify device compatibility.

## How the Instructions Page Works

The instructions page is **automatically active on all tests**. Candidates see it immediately after clicking the test link. You cannot disable this page -- it ensures every candidate understands the test rules before they begin.

The page includes:
- An explanation of which proctoring features are enabled for the test
- Browser and device requirements
- A link to a demo test so candidates can verify their setup
- Any custom instructions you add (Elite plan required)

## Adding Custom Instructions

You can include your own instructions (maximum 200 words) that appear on the instructions page alongside the default content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your test settings">
    Navigate to your test's settings page in the AutoProctor dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Advanced Settings">
    Scroll down to the **Advanced Settings** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your custom instructions">
    Type your custom instructions in the **Additional Instructions** field. You can add up to 200 words.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your settings">
    Save the test settings. Your custom instructions will now appear on the instructions page for all candidates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Gathering Additional Information from Candidates

You can use the **Gather Additional Details** feature in Advanced Settings to collect extra information from candidates. This form appears on the instructions page and candidates must complete it before starting the test.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I preview the instructions page before sharing the test link?">
    Yes -- click your own test link to see exactly what candidates will see. Viewing the instructions page does not consume a credit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does viewing the instructions page use a credit?">
    No -- credits are only deducted when a candidate successfully starts the test, not when they view the instructions page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I remove sections from the instructions page?">
    No -- the default sections are fixed and cannot be removed. You can only add additional instructions on top of the existing content.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
