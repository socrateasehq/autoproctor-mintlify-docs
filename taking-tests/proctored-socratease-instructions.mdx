Socratease quizzes are simpler to take on AutoProctor compared to Google Forms or Microsoft Forms tests. There is only **one submit button**, so there is no risk of losing your answers by clicking buttons in the wrong order.

## Try the Demo Test First

Practice with a sample proctored Socratease quiz before your actual test to verify everything works on your device.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the demo test link">
    Visit [the AutoProctor proctored demo test](https://www.autoproctor.co/tests/start-test/GFICZZZA) on the device and browser you plan to use for your actual test.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant camera and microphone access">
    When prompted, allow AutoProctor to access your camera and microphone. Proctored tests require both to function.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the demo">
    Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm that your device, browser, camera, and microphone are all compatible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Troubleshoot if needed">
    If the demo does not load, your device may not meet the minimum requirements. See [Device Compatibility](/getting-started/device-compatibility) for the full list of supported configurations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If the demo test loads successfully on your device, any Socratease quiz your administrator assigns you will load too.
</Note>

## Browser and Device Requirements

| Device | Browser | Minimum Version |
|---|---|---|
| iPhone / iPad | Safari | 13+ |
| Windows / Android / Mac | Google Chrome | 83+ |

- Update your browser to the latest version if you are running an older release.
- Your device should ideally be less than 3 years old.
- AutoProctor runs AI algorithms on your camera and audio feeds in real time, which requires significant processing power.
- Your device may handle everyday apps well but still struggle with the additional demands of real-time proctoring. If AutoProctor does not work on your device, you will need to switch to a more powerful device.

<Warning>
If the demo test fails to load on your device, your device does not support AutoProctor. You will need to use a different device with better hardware specifications.
</Warning>
