---

## Non-Socratease Quizzes (Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, etc.)

When AutoProctor embeds external forms, the candidate must **submit twice**:

1. First, submit the quiz (purple button)
2. Then, submit the proctoring session (green button)

This is because privacy restrictions prevent external platforms from notifying AutoProctor when a submission occurs.

<Warning>
If the candidate doesn't submit the quiz **first**, all answers will be lost.
</Warning>

## Socratease Quizzes

Socratease quizzes use a **single submit button**. One click submits both the answers and the proctoring session — no confusion, no risk of answer loss.

<Note>
Socratease Quizzes are available on **all plans**, including the free trial. See [Why Socratease Quiz?](/socratease/why-socratease) for more benefits.
</Note>
