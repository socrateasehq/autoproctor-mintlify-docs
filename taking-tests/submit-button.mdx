title: "Submit Button: Socratease vs Other Quizzes"
description: "Understand the key differences in how submission works between Socratease quizzes and other quiz types on AutoProctor."
---

The submission process differs depending on which quiz provider you use. Socratease quizzes are simpler and eliminate the risk of answer loss.

## Non-Socratease Quizzes (Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, etc.)

When AutoProctor embeds external forms, the candidate must **submit twice**:

1. First, submit the quiz answers (purple button)
2. Then, submit the proctoring or timed session (green button)

This two-step process exists because privacy restrictions prevent external platforms from notifying AutoProctor when a submission occurs.

<Warning>
If the candidate does not submit the quiz answers **first** (purple button), all answers will be lost. This cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

## Socratease Quizzes

Socratease quizzes use a **single submit button**. One click submits both the answers and the proctoring session — no confusion, no risk of answer loss.

<Note>
Socratease Quizzes are available on **all plans**, including the free trial. See [Why Socratease?](/socratease/why-socratease) for more benefits.
</Note>
