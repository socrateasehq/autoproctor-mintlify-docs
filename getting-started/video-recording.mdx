**tl;dr** — AutoProctor does **not** record video. Instead, it continuously monitors the candidate's camera feed in real-time.

## How AutoProctor's AI-Enabled Monitoring Works

Traditional proctoring platforms record candidate camera feeds, upload them to servers, and then process them for violation flagging. AutoProctor works differently.

Our software monitors the video feed **directly on the candidate's device** rather than uploading footage. Think of it like a smoke detector — it's on all the time, monitoring continuously, and only alerts you when something is detected.

## Key Advantages

- **No video review needed** — violations are flagged automatically with supporting evidence (photos/screenshots), so you don't need to watch hours of footage
- **Works offline** — since monitoring happens on-device, violations are detected even if the candidate disconnects from the internet
- **Reduced administrative burden** — review only flagged incidents instead of full recordings
