---
title: "Does AutoProctor Record Video?"
description: "Learn how AutoProctor monitors candidates in real-time without recording full video sessions."
---

**AutoProctor does not record video.** Instead, it continuously monitors the candidate's camera feed in real-time directly on their device and only captures evidence when a violation is detected.

## How AutoProctor's AI Monitoring Works

Traditional proctoring platforms record candidate camera feeds, upload them to servers, and then process them for violation flagging. AutoProctor works differently.

AutoProctor's software monitors the video feed **directly on the candidate's device** rather than uploading footage. Think of it like a smoke detector — it runs continuously in the background and only alerts you when something is detected.

## Key Advantages

- **No video review needed** — violations are flagged automatically with supporting evidence (photos and screenshots), so you do not need to watch hours of footage
- **Works offline** — since monitoring happens on-device, violations are detected even if the candidate temporarily disconnects from the internet
- **Reduced administrative burden** — review only flagged incidents instead of full recordings
