Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about AutoProctor below.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does AutoProctor ensure the integrity of online tests?">
    AutoProctor uses AI monitoring that accesses cameras, microphones, and screens to detect problematic behaviors like unauthorized persons or test navigation violations. These incidents generate detailed reports with evidence for test creators to review.

    Learn more about [what gets tracked](/getting-started/what-gets-tracked) during proctoring.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I create and manage tests on AutoProctor?">
    The process involves five steps:

    1. Create a quiz using Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, or [Socratease](/socratease/why-socratease)
    2. Register on AutoProctor with your test URL
    3. Configure your [proctoring settings](/creating-tests/test-settings)
    4. Share the unique test link with candidates
    5. Review [violation reports](/results/proctoring-results) afterward
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the pricing plans for AutoProctor?">
    AutoProctor offers three subscription tiers:

    - **Standard** — 150 proctored credits per billing cycle
    - **Premium** — Standard features plus team collaboration
    - **Elite** — Premium features, advanced question types, and API access

    All plans include a 10-credit free trial with no credit card required. Visit the [pricing page](https://www.autoproctor.co/pricing) for current rates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many candidates can take a proctored test simultaneously?">
    AutoProctor supports up to **10,000 concurrent candidates**. If you anticipate larger volumes, contact us in advance so we can prepare our infrastructure.

    Learn more about [concurrency limits](/results/concurrency).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does AutoProctor protect user privacy and data?">
    AutoProctor collects names, emails, images, and audio recordings exclusively for test integrity purposes and never sells data to third parties. All monitoring happens on the candidate's device — no full video recordings are uploaded to servers.

    Candidates under 18 require parental consent.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
