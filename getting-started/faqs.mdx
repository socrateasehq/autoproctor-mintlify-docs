## 1. How does AutoProctor ensure the integrity of online tests?

AutoProctor employs AI monitoring that accesses cameras, microphones, and screens to detect problematic behaviors like unauthorized persons or test navigation violations. These incidents generate detailed reports for test creators.

## 2. How can I use AutoProctor to create and manage my tests?

The process involves five steps:

1. Create a quiz via Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, or Socratease
2. Register on AutoProctor with your test URL
3. Configure proctoring settings
4. Share the unique test link with candidates
5. Review violation reports afterward

## 3. What are the pricing plans for AutoProctor?

AutoProctor offers three subscription tiers:

- **Standard** — 150 proctored attempts
- **Premium** — Standard features plus team collaboration
- **Elite** — Premium features, advanced question types, and API access

All plans include a 10-attempt free trial with cancellation flexibility.

## 4. How many candidates can take a proctored quiz simultaneously?

AutoProctor supports up to **10,000 concurrent test-takers**. Advance notice is recommended for larger volumes.

## 5. How does AutoProctor protect user privacy and data?

AutoProctor collects names, emails, images, and audio recordings exclusively for integrity purposes and never sells data to third parties. Users under 18 require parental consent.
