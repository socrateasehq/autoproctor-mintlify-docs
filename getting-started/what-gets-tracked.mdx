AutoProctor's proctoring settings are highly configurable, allowing test creators to determine which aspects of the exam environment require tracking.

## Data Feeds

AutoProctor can access the following feeds on the test-taking device:

- **Camera feed** — monitors the candidate's face and surroundings
- **Microphone feed** — detects background noise and audio cues
- **Screen share feed** — captures screen activity during the test
- **Auxiliary device camera feed** — monitors via a paired secondary device (e.g., phone)

## What We Detect and Record

Unlike traditional proctoring platforms that record full video/audio sessions, AutoProctor detects violations and shows you evidence of only those violations — so you don't spend hours reviewing each candidate's attempt.

Our tracking capabilities include:

- **Background audio detection** — recording noise and audio cues
- **Face detection** — capturing photos when no face or multiple faces are detected
- **Tab/app switching** — capturing screenshots when candidates switch tabs or applications
- **Random photos** — taking photos at random intervals throughout the exam
- **Multiple monitor detection** — identifying when additional screens are connected
- **Pre-test face capture** — taking a candidate's photo before the test starts
- **Full-screen enforcement** — ensuring the test runs in full-screen mode
- **Session action recording** — logging mouse clicks and keyboard activity
- **Auxiliary device pairing** — monitoring via a paired phone to detect keyboard usage
