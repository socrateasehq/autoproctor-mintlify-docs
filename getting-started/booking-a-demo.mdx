**You probably don't need a demo** — AutoProctor is designed to be self-serve and easy to use.

## Why Demos Usually Aren't Necessary

- The product is straightforward with transparent pricing
- Comprehensive documentation exists in article and video formats
- Our support team responds quickly to inquiries

We recommend:
1. Reading our guide on [creating tests](/creating-tests/test-settings)
2. Checking our [pricing page](https://www.autoproctor.co/pricing)
3. Trying the **free trial** — 10 test attempts, no credit card required

Plans start at **$15/month** with easy cancellation or upgrade options.

## Are There Exceptions?

Yes — if you anticipate spending **at least $2,000 over the next 3 months**, we're happy to schedule a demo. Contact us at **hello@autoproctor.co** to arrange one.
