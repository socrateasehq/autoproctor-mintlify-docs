**You probably do not need a demo.** AutoProctor is designed to be self-serve and easy to use from the start.

## Why Demos Are Usually Not Necessary

- The product is straightforward with transparent pricing
- Comprehensive documentation is available in article and video formats
- Our support team responds quickly to inquiries

<Note>
You can try AutoProctor immediately with **10 free credits** — no credit card required. Plans start at **$15/month** with easy cancellation or upgrade options.
</Note>

We recommend getting started on your own:

1. Read the guide on [configuring test settings](/creating-tests/test-settings)
2. Check the [pricing page](https://www.autoproctor.co/pricing) for plan details
3. Sign up and try the free trial — create a test and share it right away

## When a Demo Makes Sense

If you anticipate spending **at least $2,000 over the next 3 months**, we are happy to schedule a personalized demo. Contact us at **hello@autoproctor.co** to arrange one.
