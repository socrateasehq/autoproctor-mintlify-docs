## Before You Start

Make sure you have:
- An AutoProctor account (sign up at [autoproctor.co](https://www.autoproctor.co))
- A quiz ready on one of the [supported quiz providers](/creating-tests/quiz-providers) (Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, or Socratease)

<Note>
If you don't have a quiz ready, you can create one directly on AutoProctor using [Socratease](/socratease/why-socratease) — no external quiz platform needed.
</Note>

## Create Your First Test

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to AutoProctor">
    Go to [autoproctor.co](https://www.autoproctor.co) and sign in with your Google or Microsoft account. You'll land on your dashboard, where all your tests are listed.

    {/* TODO: Add screenshot of the AutoProctor dashboard for a new user */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new test">
    Click the **Create Test** button on your dashboard. You'll be asked to choose a quiz provider:

    - **Google Forms** — Use an existing Google Form
    - **Microsoft Forms** — Use an existing Microsoft Form
    - **Socratease** — Create a quiz directly on AutoProctor
    - **Iframe** — Embed any web-based quiz

    {/* TODO: Add screenshot of the quiz provider selection screen */}

    Select your quiz provider and follow the prompts to connect your quiz.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure test settings">
    After creating the test, click the **Settings** button to configure:

    - **[Timer Settings](/settings/timer-settings)** — Set a duration, start window, and submission deadline
    - **[Proctoring Settings](/settings/proctoring-settings)** — Enable camera, microphone, tab-switching detection, and more
    - **[Advanced Settings](/settings/advanced-settings)** — Choose login methods and add restrictions

    <Note>
    For your first test, the default proctoring settings work well. You can fine-tune them later based on your needs.
    </Note>

    {/* TODO: Add screenshot of the test settings page with key sections highlighted */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try the demo test">
    Before sharing with candidates, click the test link yourself to see the candidate experience. This loads a short demo that shows you exactly what your candidates will see — including the instructions page, camera check, and proctoring setup.

    {/* TODO: Add GIF showing the demo test experience */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the test link">
    Copy the test link from your dashboard or the Settings page and share it with your candidates. You can share it via:
    - Email (or use [Invite via Email](/settings/inviting-candidates-via-email) for direct invitations)
    - Your LMS or classroom platform
    - Any messaging tool

    {/* TODO: Add screenshot showing where to copy the test link */}
  </Step>
  <Step title="View results">
    Once candidates complete the test, go to your dashboard and click **Results** to see:
    - Trust scores for each candidate
    - Proctoring violation details
    - Photos, recordings, and evidence

    For a detailed guide, see [How to See Results](/results/how-to-see-results).

    {/* TODO: Add screenshot of the results page with sample data */}
  </Step>
</Steps>
