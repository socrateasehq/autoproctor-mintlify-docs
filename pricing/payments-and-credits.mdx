AutoProctor uses a credit-based billing system. Every test attempt by a candidate consumes one credit, and you purchase credits through subscriptions and optional top-ups.

## Free Credits

When you create a Test Admin account on AutoProctor, you get **10 free credits**. Each credit corresponds to a single test attempt.

For example, with 10 credits you can have 10 candidates attempt 1 test each, or 5 candidates attempt 2 tests each. Once you use up the free credits, you must purchase a subscription to continue.

## Purchase a Subscription

AutoProctor has **3 plans** (Standard, Premium, and Elite) with **3 billing frequencies** (Monthly, Quarterly, and Yearly). The more expensive the plan, the more features you get.

**Example:** If you purchase the Monthly Standard plan on 5th March, you pay X dollars. You will then be charged X dollars on the 5th of each month. If you choose a different frequency (Quarterly, Yearly), the renewal date adjusts accordingly.

<Warning>
Subscriptions auto-renew. To stop the auto-deduction, you must [cancel your subscription](/pricing/cancel-subscription).
</Warning>

When you purchase the subscription, you receive a set number of credits (for example, 150). Every time the subscription renews, the same number of credits are added to your account.

After 5 months with an active subscription, you will have accumulated **150 x 5 = 750 credits**. If 50 candidates each attempt 2 tests per month, you would use **50 x 2 x 5 = 500 credits**, leaving a balance of **250 credits**.

## Top Up Your Subscription

If you need more credits than your plan provides in a given billing cycle, you can purchase **Test Packs**. Each Test Pack contains a fixed number of credits (for example, 150), and you can buy as many as you need.

Top-ups are a **one-time cost**. The credits remain available as long as your subscription is active.

**Example:** In month 6, your class grows from 50 to 500 candidates. You now need **500 x 2 = 1,000 credits** for the month. You have 250 credits remaining and will receive 150 at renewal, totaling 400. You are short **600 credits**, which you can purchase through Test Packs.

## Credit Rollover

<Note>
As long as you maintain an active subscription (meaning you pay each month, quarter, or year), **credits do not expire -- they roll over** into subsequent billing periods.
</Note>

If you purchased 600 extra credits in month 6 but only used half of them, the remaining credits carry forward. In month 7, you would have those unused credits plus 150 from the renewal, all available for immediate use.

## Discounts

AutoProctor offers discounts on longer billing frequencies (Quarterly and Yearly vs. Monthly) as well as bulk purchases of Test Packs. Visit the [Pricing Page](https://www.autoproctor.co/pricing) for details and use the Pricing Calculator to estimate your costs.
