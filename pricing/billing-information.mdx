## Your Billing Information

<Note>
If you are part of a [Team](/pricing/teams), only your Team's Admins can access your account's Billing Information.
</Note>

All information related to your billing is available by clicking the **Billing** link on your sidebar. If you want to make changes to your company's legal information, billing address, or the credit card on file, click on the **Manage Subscription** button.

From there, you can change your:

1. **Legal entity name**
2. **Credit card details**
3. **Billing email**
4. **Billing address**
5. **GST** (only for Indian customers)

<Warning>
Any change you make here will only affect **future invoices**. If you need an amendment on an already issued invoice, send us an email at **hello@autoproctor.co** with the relevant details.
</Warning>
