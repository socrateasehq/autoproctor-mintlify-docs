## Test Attempt (Credit) Usage

Credits are added to your account when you create a subscription, when it renews, or when you purchase top-ups. Credits are then deducted as your candidates attempt tests.

You can track these deductions by clicking on the **Usage** tab in your sidebar.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/pricing/usage-sidebar-link.png" alt="Usage link on sidebar" />
</Frame>

You will see a table showing the credits used per test. Clicking on a test title will open the results for all attempts of that test.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/pricing/usage-table.png" alt="Test attempt usage table" />
</Frame>

<Note>
If a test has been shared with multiple test administrators, only the **primary admin (test creator)** will see it listed on their Usage page.
</Note>

## Team Usage

If you are part of a [Team](/pricing/teams), the Usage section displays your personal test data by default. To view team-wide usage, click the **Team Usage** button at the top right.

This will show a table with **all members of the team and the total number of credits each member has used**. You can click on an individual member to drill down into their detailed usage.
