</Note>

## What is a Team?

A Team is a set of different users (email IDs) that are linked to the same billing account. The main advantage of a Team is that **multiple users can share the same plan and billing**.

For example, if you are a school, instead of each teacher paying separately on AutoProctor, a team can be created. Anyone on the team can purchase test attempts (credits) and all the teachers can use the credits from this common pool. This can lead to **significant cost savings**.

Another advantage of a Team is that you can add members of your team as **collaborators** to your tests. The collaborators can see results, edit Socratease quiz questions, and more.

## Members

Once a team is created, you can see the different members of the team by clicking on the **Team** link on the sidebar. You will see:

- The **team name**
- The **join link** -- if an AutoProctor user visits this link, they become part of this team
- A button to **leave the team**
- A list of **all the members**
- An **"Admin" tag** showing all the Team admins

## Team Admin

A Team Admin is a team member with these additional privileges:

- Change the team name
- Make another member an admin
- Remove a member's admin privileges
- Remove a member from the team
