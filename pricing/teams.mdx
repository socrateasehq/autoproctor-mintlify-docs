The Team feature lets multiple users share a single billing account and credit pool. This is ideal for organizations where several people need to create and manage proctored tests without purchasing separate subscriptions.

<Note>
The Team feature is available only on the **Premium** and **Elite** plans.
</Note>

## What is a Team?

A Team is a group of AutoProctor users (each with their own email address) linked to the same billing account. The primary benefits are:

- **Shared billing and credits** -- Instead of each user paying separately, all team members draw from a common credit pool. Any team member can purchase credits, and the credits are available to everyone on the team.
- **Cost savings** -- Pooling credits across your organization avoids duplicate purchases and lets you take advantage of bulk discounts.
- **Test collaboration** -- You can add team members as [collaborators](/creating-tests/adding-collaborators) on your tests. Collaborators can view results, edit Socratease quiz questions, and more.

**Example:** If you are a school, instead of each teacher purchasing their own subscription, you can create a team. One purchase covers everyone, and all teachers share the same credit pool.

## Viewing Team Members

Once you create a team, click on the **Team** link in the sidebar to view the team management page. You see:

- The **team name**
- The **join link** -- any AutoProctor user who visits this link is added to the team
- A button to **leave the team**
- A list of **all team members**
- An **"Admin" tag** next to each team admin

## Team Admin Role

A Team Admin has elevated privileges beyond those of a regular team member:

| Capability | Team Admin | Regular Member |
|---|---|---|
| Rename the team | Yes | No |
| Promote members to admin | Yes | No |
| Demote other admins | Yes | No |
| Remove members from the team | Yes | No |
| Access billing information | Yes | No |
| View team-wide credit usage | Yes | No |
| Use shared credits for tests | Yes | Yes |

<Note>
Regular team members cannot access billing information or manage other members. If you need billing access, ask an existing Team Admin to promote you.
</Note>

## Creating and Joining a Team

### Creating a Team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Team section">
    Click on the **Team** link in your left sidebar. This option appears only if you are on a Premium or Elite plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your team">
    Follow the prompts to set up your team. Once created, you receive a unique join link to share with others.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Joining a Team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get the join link">
    Ask a Team Admin for the team's join link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visit the link">
    Open the join link in your browser. You are added to the team immediately and gain access to the shared credit pool.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
When you join a team, your individual subscription and credits are replaced by the team's shared subscription. If you leave the team later, you no longer have access to the team's credits.
</Warning>
