The Team feature lets multiple users share a single billing account and credit pool. This is ideal for organizations where several people need to create and manage proctored tests.

<Note>
The Team feature is available only on the **Premium** and **Elite** plans.
</Note>

## What is a Team?

A Team is a group of AutoProctor users (each with their own email address) linked to the same billing account. The primary benefits are:

- **Shared billing and credits** -- Instead of each user paying separately, all team members draw from a common credit pool. Any team member can purchase credits, and the credits are available to everyone on the team.
- **Cost savings** -- Pooling credits across your organization avoids duplicate purchases and lets you take advantage of bulk discounts.
- **Test collaboration** -- You can add team members as **collaborators** on your tests. Collaborators can view results, edit Socratease quiz questions, and more.

**Example:** If you are a school, instead of each teacher purchasing their own subscription, you can create a team. One purchase covers everyone, and all teachers share the same credit pool.

## Viewing Team Members

Once a team is created, click on the **Team** link in the sidebar to view the team management page. You will see:

- The **team name**
- The **join link** -- any AutoProctor user who visits this link will be added to the team
- A button to **leave the team**
- A list of **all team members**
- An **"Admin" tag** next to each team admin

## Team Admin Role

A Team Admin has elevated privileges beyond those of a regular team member. Specifically, a Team Admin can:

- **Rename the team** -- Change the team name at any time
- **Promote members** -- Grant admin privileges to other team members
- **Demote admins** -- Remove admin privileges from other admins
- **Remove members** -- Remove a member from the team entirely
- **Access billing** -- View and manage the [Billing Section](/pricing/billing-information), including subscription details, invoices, and payment methods
- **View team-wide usage** -- See credit consumption across all team members from the [Usage page](/pricing/track-test-pack-usage)

<Note>
Regular team members cannot access billing information or manage other members. If you need billing access, ask an existing Team Admin to promote you.
</Note>

## Creating and Joining a Team

### Creating a Team

If you are on a Premium or Elite plan, you can create a team from the **Team** section in your sidebar. Once created, you receive a unique join link to share with others.

### Joining a Team

To join an existing team, simply visit the join link provided by a Team Admin. You will be added to the team immediately and gain access to the shared credit pool.

<Warning>
When you join a team, your individual subscription and credits are replaced by the team's shared subscription. If you leave the team later, you will no longer have access to the team's credits.
</Warning>
