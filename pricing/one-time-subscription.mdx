## One-Time or Infrequent Usage

AutoProctor usage requires maintaining an active subscription, meaning monthly, quarterly, or yearly payments are necessary. However, if you only need to use AutoProctor infrequently -- for example, to conduct an exam only once a year in March -- there is a simple workaround.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a monthly subscription">
    Subscribe to a monthly plan a few days before your exam or event.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use AutoProctor for your exam">
    Conduct your exam and access all features during the subscription period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel your subscription">
    [Cancel your subscription](/pricing/cancel-subscription) a few days after your exam. You will **not** be automatically charged for the second month.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Even after cancellation, your subscription remains valid for the full month from the date of creation. So you retain full access until the billing period ends.
</Note>

The cancellation process is straightforward and takes only a few seconds. This makes it easy to use a subscription-based product like AutoProctor for one-time purposes.
