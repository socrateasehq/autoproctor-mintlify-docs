---

AutoProctor requires an active subscription to function, which means monthly, quarterly, or yearly payments. However, if you only need AutoProctor for a single event -- such as an annual exam or a one-off hiring assessment -- you can use a simple subscribe-and-cancel workflow.

## How It Works

The idea is straightforward: subscribe just before your event, use AutoProctor for your exam, and cancel shortly after. You pay for only one billing cycle.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Subscribe to a monthly plan">
    Visit the [Pricing Page](https://www.autoproctor.co/pricing) and subscribe to a monthly plan a few days before your exam. Choose the plan tier (Standard, Premium, or Elite) based on the features you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Purchase additional credits if needed">
    If the credits included with your plan are not enough, purchase additional credits through Test Packs. See [Payments and Credits Explained](/pricing/payments-and-credits) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conduct your exam">
    Create your test, share the link with candidates, and run your exam. You have full access to all features included in your plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download your results">
    After the exam, download all results and proctoring reports. Make sure you have everything you need before cancelling.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel your subscription">
    [Cancel your subscription](/pricing/cancel-subscription) a few days after the exam. The cancellation process takes only a few seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Even after cancellation, your subscription remains active for the full billing period from the date of purchase. For example, if you subscribed on March 1st, you retain full access until March 31st regardless of when you cancel.
</Note>

## Why Choose Monthly for One-Time Use

A monthly plan gives you the shortest commitment period and lowest upfront cost. You get full access to all features in your chosen tier for the entire month, which is typically more than enough time to set up, conduct, and review a single exam.

<Warning>
After your subscription expires, you will lose access to full test results and your credit balance resets to 0. Download all results before cancellation takes effect.
</Warning>
