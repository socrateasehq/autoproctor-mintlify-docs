---
title: "Purchased Packs Not Visible"
description: "Troubleshoot why purchased test packs are not showing up in your AutoProctor account."
---

Let's say you made a subscription or top-up purchase. But when you try to access your account, you don't see your account updated. There are a few possible reasons why this may happen, listed below in decreasing order of likelihood.

## 1. Logged in to a Different Account

This is **the most likely** reason. Say you bought the packs using `abc@gmail.com` and are now logged in with `xyz@school.com`. You will not be able to see your purchase reflected.

To check if this is the case:

1. Visit the **Your Account** page. You should see your email ID listed there.
2. We email customers the receipt and invoice of each purchase. Check if that email ID has received the purchase confirmation.
3. If not, identify the email ID that received the email and log in with that account.

<Note>
If you are part of a [Team](/pricing/teams), the team admin will receive the confirmation emails even if you have made the payment from your account.
</Note>

## 2. Subscription Expired

As explained in [Payments and Credits Explained](/pricing/payments-and-credits), AutoProctor is a subscription-based product. Once you finish using up your free credits, you must purchase a monthly, quarterly, or annual subscription.

When your subscription is up for renewal, we try charging your card automatically. If we are unable to charge despite multiple attempts, your subscription is cancelled and **your balance gets reset to 0**.

Read the FAQs on the [Pricing Page](https://www.autoproctor.co/pricing) to understand this better.

## 3. Associated with, or De-linked from, a Team

On AutoProctor, you can join and leave a [Team](/pricing/teams). If you are part of a Team, you share the subscription and balance with the other members of the Team. You can create a Team and be the only member in it too.

If you have left a Team by de-linking yourself from it, you will no longer see the balance of the Team. You can check your Team status by visiting the Team management page.

## 4. Error at AutoProctor's End

If you are confident that the issue is not any of the above, forward the purchase email to **hello@autoproctor.co** along with a screenshot of what you see on the Your Account page. We will check what may have happened.
