Cancelling your AutoProctor subscription takes just a few clicks. This guide walks you through the process and answers common questions about what to expect after cancellation.

## Cancel Your Subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Billing section">
    From your dashboard, click on the **Billing** link in the left sidebar.

    <Frame caption="Billing link in the sidebar.">
      <img className="max-h-[300px] border rounded-xl" src="/images/pricing/billing-sidebar-link.png" alt="Billing link highlighted in the left sidebar navigation" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Manage Subscription">
    In the **Plan Details** section, click the **Manage Subscription** button.

    <Frame caption="Manage Subscription button.">
      <img className="border rounded-xl" src="/images/pricing/manage-subscription-button.png" alt="Manage Subscription button in the Plan Details section" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel the plan">
    Click the **Cancel plan** button on the right side of the page.

    <Frame caption="Cancel plan button.">
      <img className="border rounded-xl" src="/images/pricing/cancel-plan-button.png" alt="Cancel plan button on the subscription management page" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If you are part of a [Team](/pricing/teams), only a Team Admin can cancel the subscription. Regular team members do not have access to billing controls.
</Note>

## What Happens After Cancellation

| Timing | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **Immediately after cancelling** | Your subscription is **scheduled to cancel** at the end of your current billing period. You retain full access until then. |
| **During the remaining billing period** | You and your candidates can continue using AutoProctor. You can still purchase additional credits. |
| **After the billing period ends** | Subscription status changes to **Expired**. Candidates cannot start new tests. You lose access to full results. Your credit balance resets to 0. |

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are my tests and data retained after cancellation?">
    Yes. The tests you created and their settings are **not deleted**. However, you cannot access the full results of those tests until you resubscribe.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I resubscribe after cancellation?">
    Yes. While technically you create a new subscription rather than renewing the old one, the effect is the same. You can resume using your account immediately after purchasing a new subscription.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I undo a scheduled cancellation?">
    Yes. If your subscription is scheduled to cancel but has not yet expired, you can undo the cancellation. Follow the same steps above to open **Manage Subscription** and reverse the scheduled cancellation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
Download all results and proctoring reports **before** your subscription expires. After expiration, you lose access to full test results. See questions 11-13 on the [Pricing Page](https://www.autoproctor.co/pricing) for more details.
</Warning>
