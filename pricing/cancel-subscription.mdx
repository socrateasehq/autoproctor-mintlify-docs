---

Cancelling your AutoProctor subscription is simple and takes just a few clicks. This guide walks you through the process and answers common questions about what to expect.

## Cancel Your Subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Billing section">
    From your dashboard, click on the **Billing** link in the left sidebar.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/pricing/billing-sidebar-link.png" alt="Billing link highlighted in the left sidebar navigation" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Manage Subscription">
    In the **Plan Details** section, click the **Manage Subscription** button.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/pricing/manage-subscription-button.png" alt="Manage Subscription button in the Plan Details section" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel the plan">
    Click the **Cancel plan** button on the right side of the page.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/pricing/cancel-plan-button.png" alt="Cancel plan button on the subscription management page" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If you are part of a [Team](/pricing/teams), only a Team Admin can cancel the subscription. Regular team members do not have access to billing controls.
</Note>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens immediately after I cancel?">
    Your subscription does not end right away. It is **scheduled to cancel** at the end of your current billing period.

    For example, if your monthly subscription renews on March 10th and you cancel on March 5th, your account remains fully active until March 10th. You and your candidates can continue using AutoProctor, and you can still purchase additional credits during this time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when the subscription actually expires?">
    Once the billing period ends and the subscription expires, the following changes take effect:

    - Your subscription status changes to **Expired**
    - Candidates **cannot start new test attempts**
    - You **lose access to full test results**
    - Your credit balance is **reset to 0**

    Read questions 11-13 on the [Pricing Page](https://www.autoproctor.co/pricing) for more details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are my tests and data retained?">
    Yes. The tests you created and their settings are **not deleted**. However, you will not be able to access the full results of those tests until you resubscribe.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I resubscribe after cancellation?">
    Yes. While technically you create a new subscription rather than renewing the old one, the effect is the same. You can resume using your account immediately after purchasing a new subscription.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I undo a scheduled cancellation?">
    Yes. If your subscription is scheduled to cancel but has not yet expired, you can undo the cancellation. Follow the same steps above to open **Manage Subscription** and reverse the scheduled cancellation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
