## Invoice Emails and Downloads

As discussed in [Payments and Credits Explained](/pricing/payments-and-credits), on AutoProctor you pay on:

1. **Subscription creation and renewal**
2. **Purchasing credits** (Test Packs)

For each successful payment, you are automatically emailed a copy of the **invoice** and **receipt**.

<Note>
If you are part of a [Team](/pricing/teams), the person who made the first purchase receives the email.
</Note>

If you don't see the email, you (and all Team Admins) can download the invoice from the [Billing Section](https://www.autoproctor.co/test-admin/billing/). You can learn more in the [Editing Your Billing Information](/pricing/billing-information) article.
