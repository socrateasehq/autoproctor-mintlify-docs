---
title: "Incorrect Student Name in Results"
description: "Fix incorrect student names showing in the results page or Excel export."
---

AutoProctor does not request name input from users directly. If a student's name appears incorrectly on results, it is because of one of the following reasons:

1. The name they have chosen on their Google Account is wrong.
2. They have used the wrong email ID while attempting the test.

<img src="/images/candidate-issues/incorrect-student-name.png" alt="Example of incorrect student name in AutoProctor results" />

## How to fix it

To correct the name in reports, the student must update their profile on AutoProctor. Once they modify their name, the change automatically reflects across all reports.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the student's email">
    Find the email address associated with the student's test attempt in the results page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Direct the student to update their profile">
    Have the student navigate to [https://www.autoproctor.co/account/edit-profile](https://www.autoproctor.co/account/edit-profile) and update their name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Changes sync automatically">
    Once the student updates their name on their profile, the change will automatically be reflected across all existing test reports.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
For example, if a report shows "Cedric Daniels" but the correct name is "Jimmy McNulty" for the account jimmy@thewire.com, the student needs to visit the profile edit page and update their name. The change will then appear on all reports automatically.
</Note>
