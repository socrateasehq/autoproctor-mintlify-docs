---
title: "Incorrect Candidate Name in Results"
description: "Fix incorrect candidate names showing in the results page or Excel export. Names are pulled from the candidate's linked Google or Microsoft account."
---

AutoProctor does not ask candidates to type their name directly. Instead, it pulls the name from the account used to sign in. If a candidate's name appears incorrectly in results, the issue originates from their account profile.

## Why does this happen?

AutoProctor retrieves candidate names from the authentication provider they used to sign in:

- **Google account** -- the display name set in [Google Account settings](https://myaccount.google.com/personal-info)
- **Microsoft account** -- the display name set in [Microsoft Account settings](https://account.microsoft.com/profile)

If the name on their Google or Microsoft account is incorrect, misspelled, or uses a nickname, that same name will appear in AutoProctor results.

<Note>
AutoProctor pulls candidate names directly from their Google or Microsoft account profile. If the name is wrong in results, the candidate needs to update their profile -- either on their Google/Microsoft account or on AutoProctor directly.
</Note>

## How to fix it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the candidate's email">
    Find the email address associated with the candidate's test attempt on the results page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Direct the candidate to update their profile">
    Have the candidate visit [https://www.autoproctor.co/account/edit-profile](https://www.autoproctor.co/account/edit-profile) and update their name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the change">
    Once the candidate updates their name on their profile, the change automatically reflects across all existing test reports. No further action is needed from the test administrator.
  </Step>
</Steps>
