---
title: "Missing Random Photos"
description: "Understand why random photos may not appear in a candidate's proctoring report."
---

Random photos are taken at random times throughout the test. The system selects unpredictable moments during the exam to capture photos of the test taker.

## Why are random photos missing?

If a test has a duration of 5 minutes, the system may decide to take 3 photos at 2:00, 4:15, and 4:45. If the candidate submits their test at 1:30, the system hasn't had enough time to take the first photo. So, you won't find any random photos in the report.

<Note>
Random photos will only appear if the candidate was actively taking the test at the time the system scheduled the photo captures. If the test was submitted before any scheduled captures, no random photos will be available.
</Note>
