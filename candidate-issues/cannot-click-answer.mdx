---
title: "Cannot Click on Answer"
description: "Fix the issue where the test form loads but you cannot click on questions or answers."
---

If you see a form like the one below, where you cannot click on the questions or select answers, this is typically caused by being logged into the wrong Google account.

<img src="/images/candidate-issues/cannot-click-form.png" alt="Form that cannot be clicked due to incorrect Google account" />

## How to fix it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log out of all Google accounts">
    Visit [https://accounts.google.com/Logout](https://accounts.google.com/Logout) and log out of all your accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with the correct Gmail account">
    Sign back in with the correct Gmail account that has been granted access to the test.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload the test">
    Try loading the test again after signing in with the correct account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
