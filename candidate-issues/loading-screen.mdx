title: "Test Stuck at Loading Screen"
description: "Fix tests that are stuck on the loading screen due to device or browser compatibility issues."
---

AutoProctor uses quite modern technologies to detect and analyse anomalies. This requires modern browsers and adequate device hardware specifications.

## Why is my test stuck at loading?

AutoProctor employs advanced technologies for proctoring, which means your browser and device need to meet certain minimum requirements. If the test is stuck at the loading screen, it typically means your device or browser is not fully compatible.

<img src="/images/candidate-issues/loading-screen-demo.png" alt="AutoProctor loading screen" />

## How to fix it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Try the demo test">
    Take the demo test to check if your device and browser are compatible with AutoProctor. This helps confirm whether the issue is with your device setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use a modern browser">
    Ensure you are using the latest version of a modern browser such as Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Firefox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your device">
    If the test doesn't load despite you following all the instructions, it means that your device isn't supported, and you will need a higher-end device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
If the test does not load even after following the steps above, your device does not meet the minimum hardware requirements for AutoProctor. You will need to use a different, higher-end device.
</Warning>
