---
title: "Blank Page or Grey Screen"
description: "Resolve blank page or grey screen issues when loading a test on AutoProctor."
---

If your test is showing a blank page or grey screen, the most common cause is that you are logged into the wrong Google account -- one that does not have access to the test.

<img src="/images/candidate-issues/blank-page-google-account.png" alt="Blank page due to incorrect Google account" />

## How to fix it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log out of all Google accounts">
    Visit [https://accounts.google.com/Logout](https://accounts.google.com/Logout) to log out of all your Google accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign back in with the correct account">
    Sign in with the Google account that has been granted access to the test.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reload the test">
    Navigate back to the test link and try loading it again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
For detailed instructions on setting up your Google account before taking a test, refer to the following guides:
- [Instructions for Taking a Proctored Test](/taking-tests/proctored-test-instructions) -- see the Google Accounts section
- [Instructions for Taking a Timed Test](/taking-tests/timed-test-instructions) -- see the Google Accounts section
</Note>
