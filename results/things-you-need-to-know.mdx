Hundreds of thousands of candidates have used AutoProctor successfully. However, common mistakes can lead to test access failures or lost submissions. Follow these five requirements to avoid issues.

<Warning>
Improper use of AutoProctor can result in candidates being unable to take tests or losing their submitted answers. Read this page carefully before administering your first test.
</Warning>

## 1. No File Upload Questions

File uploads are not compatible with AutoProctor. Do not include file upload question types in your Google Forms. All other question formats are supported.

## 2. Share Instructions with Candidates

Almost all issues candidates face can be avoided if they read and follow instructions beforehand. Share the appropriate instructions page based on your test configuration:

- For tests with only a timer, share the [timed test instructions](/taking-tests/timed-test-instructions)
- For tests with proctoring, share the [proctored test instructions](/taking-tests/proctored-test-instructions)

<Note>
Have your candidates complete a demo test at least **24 hours** before their actual exam. This helps them understand the platform and verifies their device is compatible. See [Best Practices](/results/best-practices-for-teachers) for the demo link.
</Note>

## 3. Click Both Submit Buttons

Candidates must click two submit buttons -- first the one within the test form (Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, or Socratease), and then the green **Submit** button within AutoProctor.

<Warning>
If candidates do not click the green **Submit** button in AutoProctor, **all their answers may be lost**. This cannot be recovered. Share the [submit button guide](/taking-tests/submit-button) with your candidates.
</Warning>

## 4. Log Out of Other Google Accounts

Candidates experiencing blank pages, connection errors, or inability to select answers should log out of all their other Google accounts before attempting the test. This resolves the majority of technical issues.

For more troubleshooting help, see:
- [Blank Page / Grey Screen](/candidate-issues/blank-page-grey-screen)
- [Cannot Click Answers](/candidate-issues/cannot-click-answer)
- [Loading Screen Issues](/candidate-issues/loading-screen)

## 5. Maintain Sufficient Test Attempts

AutoProctor requires paid credits to operate. You must ensure your account has enough test attempts available, or candidates cannot load the test. See [Payments and Credits](/pricing/payments-and-credits) for details on purchasing and tracking credits.
