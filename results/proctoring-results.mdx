## Viewing proctoring results

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the results page">
    Navigate to your dashboard and click the **Results** button corresponding to the test you want to review.

    ![Results button on dashboard](/images/results/results-button-dashboard.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the results table">
    The results page displays a table containing all submissions. You can see when the test taker started and finished the test, along with their trust score. For Socratease Quizzes, quiz scores are also visible.

    ![Results table](/images/results/results-table.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="View the proctoring summary">
    Click on the **Trust Score** to view the proctoring summary. This page lists any violations committed during the test.

    ![Proctoring summary](/images/results/proctoring-summary.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
