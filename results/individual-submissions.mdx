---
title: "Checking Individual Submissions"
description: "View individual candidate submissions, scores, and responses on AutoProctor for Socratease Quizzes and Google Forms tests."
---

After candidates complete a test, you can review their individual submissions to see detailed responses, scores, and grading status. The process differs depending on which quiz platform you use.

## For Socratease Quizzes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your dashboard">
    Visit your AutoProctor dashboard and locate the test you want to review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click View under Results">
    Click the **View** button under the **Results** section of the relevant test. This opens a list of all candidate submissions.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/results/submissions-list.png" alt="List of candidate submissions showing names, scores, and grading status" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check scores and responses">
    Each submission shows one of two states:

    - **Score link with percentage** -- The candidate has finished the test and it has been graded. Click the score link to view their full responses.
    - **Grade button in blue** -- The candidate has not finished the test, or it has not yet been graded. Click **Grade** to review and grade their responses.

    Clicking either link opens a detailed page showing all responses, points scored, and candidate-specific information.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/results/individual-submission-details.png" alt="Individual submission detail page showing candidate responses and points scored" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Google Forms

Google Forms manages its own response data independently from AutoProctor. To view individual candidate responses in Google Forms:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your Google Form">
    Go to the Google Form you used for the test.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Responses tab">
    Select the **Responses** tab at the top of the form editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View individual responses">
    Click the **Individual** tab to browse through each candidate's responses one at a time. You can navigate between submissions using the arrow buttons.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For more details on Google Forms response features, see [Google's official documentation](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/139706).

<Note>
If you cannot see responses in Google Forms, refer to our guide on [missing Google Forms responses](/candidate-issues/google-forms-response-not-visible).
</Note>
