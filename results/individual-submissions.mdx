title: "Checking Individual Submissions"

---

## For Socratease Quizzes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your dashboard">
    Visit your dashboard and locate the test you want to review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click View under Results">
    Click the **View** button under the **Results** section of the relevant test. This opens a list of user submissions.

    ![Submissions list](/images/results/submissions-list.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check scores and responses">
    - If the user has finished the test and it has been graded, their score appears as a link with a percentage next to it (highlighted in the red box in the image).
    - If the user has not finished the test or it has not been graded, you will see the word **Grade** in blue.

    Clicking on the blue link opens a page showing all the responses of the user, points scored, and other candidate-specific information.

    ![Individual submission details](/images/results/individual-submission-details.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For Google Forms Quizzes

For Google Forms quizzes, follow [Google's official documentation](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/139706) for checking individual responses and learning more about Google Form quiz features.
