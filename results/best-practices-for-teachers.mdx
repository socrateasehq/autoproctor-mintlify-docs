Smooth test administration requires preparation. Follow these best practices to minimize technical issues and ensure your candidates have a successful testing experience.

## Pre-Test Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Have candidates complete a demo test">
    Share the demo test link with candidates at least **24 hours** before the actual exam. The demo helps candidates understand the platform and confirms their device meets technical requirements.

    Demo link: [https://www.autoproctor.co/tests/start-test/GFICZZZA](https://www.autoproctor.co/tests/start-test/GFICZZZA)

    <Note>
    The demo test does not consume test attempts from your account. Candidates can take it as many times as they need.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share instructions with candidates">
    Distribute the appropriate instructions page based on your test configuration:

    - [Proctored test instructions](/taking-tests/proctored-test-instructions) -- For tests with proctoring enabled
    - [Timed test instructions](/taking-tests/timed-test-instructions) -- For tests with only a timer
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your account has sufficient credits">
    Ensure your account has enough test attempts available. If candidates try to load a test and your account has no credits, they cannot proceed. See [Payments and Credits](/pricing/payments-and-credits).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure test timing with buffer">
    When scheduling timed tests with both a duration and a time window, allocate extra buffer time. For example, if your test runs for 15 minutes, set the window to at least 30 minutes to account for candidate setup and technical delays.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Distributing Test Links

- Use **Gmail** or **Google Classroom** to distribute test links. Avoid sharing links through messaging apps (such as Telegram or Facebook) whose in-app browsers may cause compatibility issues.
- If candidates receive links through messaging apps, advise them to copy the link and open it directly in **Chrome** or **Firefox**.
- Send the demo link at least 24 hours before the actual test, and the real test link closer to the scheduled time.

## During the Test

- **Be available** -- Remain accessible via a chat application (such as Google Meet or WhatsApp) during testing so candidates can reach you if they encounter technical issues.
- **Monitor results** -- Keep your AutoProctor dashboard open to monitor submissions as they come in.

## After the Test

- **Review proctoring results** -- Check [Trust Scores](/getting-started/trust-score) and proctoring reports for each candidate.
- **Clarify result sources** -- AutoProctor provides Trust Scores only. Actual grades and scores appear in your quiz platform (Google Forms, Microsoft Forms, or Socratease). Make sure candidates understand that:
  - Technical issues should be directed to AutoProctor support
  - Academic questions and grade inquiries should be directed to you, the test creator
