---
title: "Share Test Results with Other Users"
description: "Grant other administrators or colleagues access to test results on AutoProctor, including methods for all supported quiz platforms."
---

You may need to share test results with other administrators, co-instructors, or colleagues who did not create the original test. The method depends on which quiz platform you use.

## For Google Forms

When you share a Google Form with another user, they can gain access to the same AutoProctor test and its results.

<Frame caption="Opening the Timer + Proctor add-on on a shared Google Form.">
  <video controls className="w-full rounded-xl" src="/videos/settings/timer-proctor-on-gf.mp4"></video>
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share the Google Form">
    Share the Google Form link with the users who need access. Make sure they have **Editor** permissions on the form itself.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Timer + Proctor add-on">
    Have the other users open the **Timer + Proctor** add-on from within the shared Google Form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify access">
    Once they open the add-on, the other users become associated with the same test. They see the same test link and can view results on their AutoProctor dashboard.

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The other users must open the Timer + Proctor add-on at least once from within the shared Google Form. Simply sharing the Google Form link is not enough -- they must launch the add-on to be associated with the test.
</Note>

## For Socratease Quizzes

Use the **Collaborators** feature to grant other users access to your test and its results.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your test settings">
    Navigate to the test on your AutoProctor dashboard and open its settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add collaborators">
    Add the email addresses of users you want to grant access to. See [Adding Collaborators](/creating-tests/adding-collaborators) for detailed instructions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Collaborators can view results, proctoring reports, and manage the test depending on the permissions you assign.

## For All Test Types

You can also share results by exporting them:

| Method | Description |
|---|---|
| **Excel export** | Download results and share the file directly. See [Export to Excel](/results/export-to-excel) |
| **Google Sheets** | Set up automatic export to a shared Google Sheet. See [Google Sheets Integration](/results/google-sheets-integration) |
