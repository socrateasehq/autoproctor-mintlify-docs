---
title: "How to Allow Other Users to Access Test Results"
description: "Share test results access with other teachers or administrators on AutoProctor."
---

If you need to grant other users (such as other teachers or administrators) access to test results after students have completed a test, follow these steps.

<Note>
This process is designed for Google Forms users.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share the Google Form link">
    Share the Google Form link with the other users who need access to the results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Timer + Proctor add-on">
    Have the other users open the **Timer + Proctor** add-on from within the Google Form itself.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once these steps are completed, the other users become associated with the same Google Form. They will see the same test link and be able to edit and manage the Google Form in exactly the same way as you.
