---
title: "How to Restrict AutoProctor to Only Some Users"
description: "Learn how to limit test participation to specific students on AutoProctor."
---

Students access AutoProctor tests through a shared link, and anyone with the link can take the test. However, there are scenarios where you may need to limit participation to specific students.

## Method 1: Selective link distribution

The simplest approach is to ensure that the test link is not available to all users. If your class has 20 students but only 15 should take the test, share the link exclusively with those 15 students.

## Method 2: Google Form access restrictions

If you are concerned about students sharing the link with others, you can restrict the Google Form itself rather than the AutoProctor link. While students can still open the AutoProctor link, they will not be able to proceed if Google Form access is blocked. This effectively prevents test completion without blocking the initial link access.

For implementation details on restricting access to Google Forms, refer to [this guide on restricting Google Form access](https://www.bettercloud.com/monitor/the-academy/restrict-access-to-google-forms/).
