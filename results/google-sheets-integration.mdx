---
title: "Automatically Write Test Results to Google Sheets"
description: "Set up automatic export of AutoProctor test results to Google Sheets instead of manually downloading Excel files."
---

<Note>
This is a **Premium Feature** and requires a Premium or Elite subscription.
</Note>

Instead of manually downloading Excel files, you can configure AutoProctor to automatically write test results to a Google Sheet.

## Setup instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open test settings">
    Visit the **Test Settings** for a new or existing test.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Google Sheets ID field">
    Scroll down to the **Advanced Settings** section where it says **Google Sheets ID**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a blank Google Sheet">
    Create an empty Google Sheet on your Google Drive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant write access">
    Share the Google Sheet with `hello@autoproctor.co` and grant it **Editor** permissions so AutoProctor can write data to the sheet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste the Google Sheet URL">
    Copy the URL of the Google Sheet and paste it into the **Google Sheets ID** field in AutoProctor's Test Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the test">
    Click **Create** or **Update** to save your test configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the integration">
    Complete a test attempt. A new sheet labeled **AutoProctor** will automatically appear in your Google Sheet with the results data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Subsequent test attempts will automatically populate the same Google Sheet without requiring any additional setup.

## Socratease Quiz integration

For Socratease Quiz integrations, the system writes both proctoring scores and quiz scores to the sheet. However, individual questions and answers are not included -- only aggregate scoring is displayed.
