---
title: "View Details of Unsubmitted Tests"
description: "Access and review information about tests that candidates started but did not submit, including proctoring data and attempt timestamps."
---

An unsubmitted test occurs when a candidate starts a test but does not click the green **Submit** button at the top of the test window. The candidate may have viewed questions and entered answers, but their test was never formally submitted. You can still access proctoring data and attempt details for these incomplete sessions.

<Note>
Use **Method 1** to see all unsubmitted tests across a test at a glance. Use **Method 2** when you are already reviewing a specific candidate's proctoring report and want to check their unsubmitted attempts.
</Note>

## Method 1: From the General Test Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the results page">
    Navigate to your test on the AutoProctor dashboard and click **Results**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the Unsubmitted Tests filter">
    Locate the **Unsubmitted Tests** checkbox on the results page and check it. All unsubmitted tests then appear alongside submitted results in the listing.

## Method 2: From an Individual Candidate Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a candidate's proctoring report">
    From the results page, click on a candidate's **Trust Score** to open their proctoring report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the unsubmitted attempts button">
    Look for a button indicating the number of unsubmitted attempts. Click it to reveal the details of those incomplete sessions.
