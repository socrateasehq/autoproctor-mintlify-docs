---
title: "How to See Details of Unsubmitted Tests"
description: "Access and view information about tests that candidates started but did not submit."
---

An unsubmitted test occurs when a candidate does not click the green **Submit** button at the top of the test. The test taker may have started the test and viewed questions but failed to submit their answers.

## Viewing unsubmitted tests

There are two methods for accessing unsubmitted test data.

### Method 1: General test report

Locate the **Unsubmitted Tests** checkbox on your test report page and check it. Any unsubmitted tests will then display in the listing.

![Unsubmitted tests checkbox](/images/results/unsubmitted-checkbox.png)

### Method 2: Proctored test reports

When reviewing a specific student's test attempt report, a button shows the quantity of unsubmitted attempts. Clicking this button reveals those particular results.

![Unsubmitted attempts button](/images/results/unsubmitted-attempts-button.png)

![Unsubmitted test details](/images/results/unsubmitted-test-details.png)
