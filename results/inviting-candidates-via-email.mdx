title: "Inviting Candidates via Email"
description: "Generate unique invitation links tied to specific email addresses for controlled test access."
---

<Note>
This feature is available only on the **Elite Plan** and works exclusively with **Socratease Quizzes** and **IFrame Tests**. It is not compatible with Google Forms or Microsoft Forms, as those require Login with Google/Microsoft.
</Note>

## How it works

This feature enables controlled test access by generating unique invitation links tied to specific email addresses. When candidates access their unique link, the system sends a one-time verification code to their associated email address before test access is granted.

## Setup process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Unique URL">
    Enable the **Unique URL** option within your test settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload email IDs">
    Upload email IDs via a CSV file. You can upload a maximum of 1,000 email IDs at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get the generated links">
    You will receive a text output containing email IDs and their corresponding test links as columns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Format the data">
    Paste the data into a spreadsheet, splitting on the comma separator to organize email addresses and links into columns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Distribute links">
    Send the unique links to your candidates via your preferred method.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important limitations

- **Direct URL disabled** -- Once you enable this feature, the direct (common) URL is no longer valid. Only the unique per-candidate URLs will work.
- **No revocation** -- It is currently not possible to invalidate sent invitations.
- **No free trial** -- Testing this feature before purchasing the Elite Plan is not available.

## Bulk email service

For large-scale campaigns exceeding 5,000 candidates, AutoProctor offers an email distribution service at $100 per 5,000 messages. This requires sufficient account balance and submission of CSV and email content.
