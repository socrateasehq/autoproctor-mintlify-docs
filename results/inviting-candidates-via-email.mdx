---
title: "Inviting Candidates via Email"
description: "Generate unique invitation links tied to specific email addresses for controlled test access with email verification on AutoProctor."
---

This feature enables controlled test access by generating unique URLs tied to specific email addresses. When candidates open their unique link, AutoProctor sends a one-time verification code to their email before granting access to the test.

<Note>
This feature is available only on the **Elite Plan** and works exclusively with **Socratease Quizzes** and **IFrame Tests**. It is not compatible with Google Forms or Microsoft Forms, as those require platform-specific sign-in. See [Payments and Credits](/pricing/payments-and-credits) for plan details.
</Note>

## Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Unique URL">
    Open your test settings and enable the **Unique URL** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload email addresses">
    Upload candidate email addresses via a CSV file. You can upload a maximum of 1,000 email addresses at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get the generated links">
    AutoProctor generates a text output containing email addresses and their corresponding unique test links as comma-separated columns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Format the data">
    Paste the output into a spreadsheet application and split on the comma separator to organize email addresses and links into separate columns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Distribute links">
    Send the unique links to your candidates via email, your learning management system, or another distribution method.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important Limitations

- **Direct URL disabled** -- Once you enable this feature, the standard shared test URL is no longer valid. Only the unique per-candidate URLs work.
- **No revocation** -- It is currently not possible to invalidate sent invitations after they are generated.
- **No free trial** -- This feature cannot be tested before purchasing the Elite Plan.

<Warning>
After enabling unique URLs, the common test link stops working immediately. Make sure you are ready to distribute the individual links before enabling this feature.
</Warning>

## Bulk Email Service

For large-scale campaigns exceeding 5,000 candidates, AutoProctor offers an email distribution service at $100 per 5,000 messages. This requires sufficient account balance and submission of your CSV file and email content to the AutoProctor team.
