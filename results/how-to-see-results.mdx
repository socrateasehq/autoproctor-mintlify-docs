---
title: "How to See Results on AutoProctor"
description: "Understand the two types of results available on AutoProctor: test results and proctoring results."
---

There are two types of results that can be seen for any test, and they are distinct from each other.

## Test results

These are the results of any tests the student took. Test creators can check these results to understand the student's performance on the tests.

<Note>
Unless you are using Socratease Quizzes, you cannot see test results on AutoProctor.
</Note>

Test results appear in your original platform:

- **Google Forms** -- Check the **Responses** tab in Google Forms
- **Microsoft Forms** -- Results appear in Microsoft Forms as they normally would
- **IFrame tests** -- Results appear in whatever platform you embedded

If you are a Google Forms user and are not able to see results, please refer to our troubleshooting guide for missing Google Forms responses.

## Proctoring results

As the test taker attempts the test, AutoProctor monitors their test-taking environment and records evidence of possible cheating or malpractice. This information is compiled into a report.

Proctoring results are only available when proctoring is enabled in your test settings. To learn how to view proctoring results, see [Where can I see proctoring results?](/results/proctoring-results).
