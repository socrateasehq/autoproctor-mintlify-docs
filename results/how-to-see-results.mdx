title: "How to See Results on AutoProctor"
description: "Understand the two types of results available on AutoProctor -- test results showing candidate performance and proctoring results tracking integrity."
---

AutoProctor provides two distinct types of results for every test: **test results** (how candidates performed) and **proctoring results** (whether candidates maintained integrity). Knowing the difference helps you find the right data quickly.

## Test Results

Test results show each candidate's answers and scores. Unless you use Socratease Quizzes, these results appear in your original quiz platform -- not on the AutoProctor dashboard.

<Note>
Unless you use Socratease Quizzes, you cannot view test results directly on AutoProctor. Test results appear in the platform where you created the test.
</Note>

| Quiz Platform | Where to Find Test Results |
|---|---|
| **Google Forms** | Open your Google Form and click the **Responses** tab to see all submissions |
| **Microsoft Forms** | Check the **Responses** section in Microsoft Forms as you normally would |
| **IFrame Tests** | View results in the platform you embedded within the IFrame |
| **Socratease Quizzes** | View results directly on your AutoProctor dashboard under the test's **Results** section |

<Note>
If you use Google Forms and cannot see responses, refer to the troubleshooting guide on [missing Google Forms responses](/candidate-issues/google-forms-response-not-visible).
</Note>

## Proctoring Results

As a candidate takes the test, AutoProctor monitors their testing environment and records evidence of possible cheating or malpractice. This data compiles into a proctoring report with a [Trust Score](/getting-started/trust-score).

<Note>
Proctoring results are only available when you enable proctoring in your [test settings](/settings/proctoring-settings). Tests configured with only a timer do not generate proctoring reports.
</Note>
