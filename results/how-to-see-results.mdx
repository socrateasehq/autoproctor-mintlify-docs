description: "Understand the two types of results available on AutoProctor -- test results showing candidate performance and proctoring results tracking integrity."
---

AutoProctor provides two distinct types of results for every test: test results and proctoring results. Understanding the difference helps you find the information you need quickly.

## Test Results

Test results show how each candidate performed on the test itself, including their answers and scores. These results appear in your original quiz platform, not on the AutoProctor dashboard.

<Note>
Unless you are using Socratease Quizzes, you cannot view test results directly on AutoProctor. Test results appear in the platform where you created the test.
</Note>

Where to find test results by platform:

- **Google Forms** -- Open your Google Form and click the **Responses** tab to see all submissions
- **Microsoft Forms** -- Check the **Responses** section in Microsoft Forms as you normally would
- **IFrame Tests** -- View results in the platform you embedded within the IFrame
- **Socratease Quizzes** -- View results directly on your AutoProctor dashboard under the test's **Results** section

If you use Google Forms and cannot see responses, refer to our troubleshooting guide on [missing Google Forms responses](/candidate-issues/google-forms-response-not-visible).

## Proctoring Results

As a candidate takes the test, AutoProctor monitors their testing environment and records evidence of possible cheating or malpractice. This information is compiled into a proctoring report with a [Trust Score](/getting-started/trust-score).

<Note>
Proctoring results are only available when proctoring is enabled in your [test settings](/creating-tests/test-settings). Tests configured with only a timer do not generate proctoring reports.
</Note>

To learn how to access proctoring results, see [Where can I see proctoring results?](/results/proctoring-results).
