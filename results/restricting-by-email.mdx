---
title: "Restricting the Test Based on Email ID"
description: "Limit test access to specific email domains or individual email addresses using Login Restrictions."
---

You can control who takes your tests by restricting access based on email criteria. This is done through the **Login Restrictions** section in **Advanced Settings**.

## Domain-based restrictions

You can set restrictions to allow only email addresses ending with specific domains. For example, you can restrict access to only users with `@abc.com` and `@xyz.com` email addresses.

![Login restrictions by domain](/images/results/login-restrictions-domain.png)

## Specific email restrictions

Beyond domain limitations, you can also permit individual email addresses. This allows you to combine domain-level and individual-level restrictions. For example, you can allow all users whose email ends with `@abc.com` as well as specific individual email addresses.

![Login restrictions with specific email IDs](/images/results/login-restrictions-email.png)
