## What is a Socratease Quiz?

Socratease is AutoProctor's proprietary quizzing platform. It is designed to be as easy to use as Google Forms or Microsoft Forms, but is specifically engineered for online examinations. Key highlights include:

- A **single [submit button](/taking-tests/submit-button)** -- no confusion between submitting the quiz and the proctored test
- **Integrated creation and results** viewing within one dashboard
- Support for **multiple question formats** alongside advanced capabilities like Excel imports and question banks

## How to Create a Socratease Quiz

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to AutoProctor">
    Visit [autoproctor.co](https://www.autoproctor.co) and log in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Test">
    Click on **Create Test** and select the **Socratease Quizzes** option as your quiz provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Questions">
    Add your questions using any of the [supported question types](/socratease/question-types). Configure points, time limits, and other settings for each question.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set up your [quiz settings](/socratease/quiz-settings) including question display mode, result visibility, randomization, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the Test">
    Share the test link with your candidates. They can access the quiz through the link you provide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Results">
    After candidates complete the test, view their results and proctoring reports from the same dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
