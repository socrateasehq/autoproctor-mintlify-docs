**You probably do not need a demo.** AutoProctor is designed to be self-serve and easy to use from the start. Most users are up and running within minutes.

## Why Demos Are Usually Not Necessary

- The product is straightforward with transparent pricing
- Comprehensive documentation covers every feature in detail
- Our support team responds quickly to inquiries at **hello@autoproctor.co**

<Note>
You can try AutoProctor immediately with **10 free credits** -- no credit card required. Plans start at **$15/month** with easy [cancellation](/pricing/cancel-subscription) or upgrade options.
</Note>

## Getting Started on Your Own

We recommend trying AutoProctor directly:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the documentation">
    Read the [Things You Need to Know](/results/things-you-need-to-know) page and check the [pricing page](https://www.autoproctor.co/pricing) for plan details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign up for free">
    Create an account and receive 10 free test credits. No credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and share a test">
    Set up a test, share the link with candidates, and see results on your dashboard. The entire process takes just a few minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When a Demo Makes Sense

If you anticipate spending **at least $2,000 over the next 3 months**, we are happy to schedule a personalized demo. Contact us at **hello@autoproctor.co** to arrange one.
