AutoProctor is an AI proctoring platform that monitors candidates during online assessments. You bring your own quiz, and AutoProctor handles the proctoring. When you create a test, you first select a quiz provider, then configure questions, points, timers, and proctoring settings.

## Choosing a Quiz Provider

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Test">
    Go to your **Dashboard** and click **Create Test**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Quiz Provider">
    Choose one of the available providers: **Socratease Quizzes**, **Google Forms**, **Microsoft Forms**, or **IFrame/Other**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your Quiz">
    Set up your questions, points, and any provider-specific settings. Then configure [timer](/settings/timer-settings) and [proctoring](/settings/proctoring-settings) settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available Quiz Providers

| Provider | Best For | Key Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| **Socratease Quizzes** | Full-featured assessments | Native integration with auto-submit protection |
| **Google Forms** | Google Classroom users | Dedicated add-on for automatic test creation |
| **Microsoft Forms** | Microsoft ecosystem organizations | Seamless integration for enterprise evaluations |
| **IFrame/Other** | TypeForm, ProProfs, ClassMarker, etc. | Adds proctoring to any web-based quiz platform |

### Socratease Quizzes

Socratease is AutoProctor's native quizzing tool. It provides the tightest integration and the best candidate experience:

- **Single submit button** prevents answer loss from incorrect sequencing
- **Auto-submit protection** saves answers before the test closes
- **Multiple question types** including MCQs, essays, voice responses, coding, and more
- **Excel bulk import** and question banks for fast quiz assembly

### IFrame/Other Providers

Hundreds of quiz platforms exist, including TypeForm, ProProfs, and ClassMarker. While these platforms do not have built-in remote proctoring, you can add AutoProctor's proctoring to any of them by selecting the **IFrame/Other** option. AutoProctor embeds your quiz inside a proctored browser window.

<Note>
When using IFrame/Other providers, you can customize the embedded URL using [query arguments](/creating-tests/iframe-query-arguments).
</Note>
