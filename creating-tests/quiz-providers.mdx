AutoProctor is an AI proctoring platform — it monitors candidates during online assessments. You bring your own quiz, and AutoProctor handles the proctoring.

When setting up a test, you first select your quiz provider, then configure questions, points, timers, and proctoring settings.

## Available Quiz Providers

### Socratease Quizzes

AutoProctor's native quizzing tool. Recommended for:
- **Single submit button** — prevents answer loss from incorrect sequencing
- **Auto-submit protection** — answers are saved before the test closes
- **Multiple question types** — MCQs, essays, voice responses, coding, and more
- **Excel bulk import** and question banks

### Google Forms

The most widely-used quizzing platform, particularly popular through Google Classrooms. AutoProctor offers a dedicated **Google Forms add-on** for automatic test creation.

### Microsoft Forms

The second most-used platform, especially within Microsoft ecosystem organizations for evaluations and hiring assessments.

### Other Providers

Platforms like TypeForm, ProProfs, and ClassMarker can integrate with AutoProctor's proctoring using the **IFrame/Other** option — even though they don't have built-in remote proctoring.
