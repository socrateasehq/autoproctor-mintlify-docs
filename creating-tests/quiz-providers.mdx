AutoProctor is an AI proctoring platform — it monitors candidates during online assessments. You bring your own quiz, and AutoProctor handles the proctoring. When setting up a test, you first select your quiz provider, then configure questions, points, timers, and proctoring settings.

## Available Quiz Providers

### Socratease Quizzes

AutoProctor's native quizzing tool. Recommended for the best experience:

- **Single submit button** — prevents answer loss from incorrect sequencing
- **Auto-submit protection** — answers are saved before the test closes
- **Multiple question types** — MCQs, essays, voice responses, coding, and more
- **Excel bulk import** and question banks

Platforms like TypeForm, ProProfs, and ClassMarker can integrate with AutoProctor's proctoring using the **IFrame/Other** option — even though they do not have built-in remote proctoring.
