---

Once a test is created, you can add collaborators through the **Advanced Settings** section.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/creating-tests/collaborators-section.png" alt="Collaborators section in Advanced Settings" />
</Frame>

<Note>
Only **Premium** and **Elite** plan users can add collaborators. Collaborators must already be members of your team.
</Note>

## How to Add a Collaborator

1. Navigate to **Advanced Settings** on your test
2. Click the **collaborate link** to copy it
3. Share the link with the intended collaborator
4. When the person clicks the link, they gain access to the test

## Important Considerations

- **Full access** — once added, collaborators have read and edit access to the test. They can change settings, view results, etc.
- **Permanent** — currently, there is no way to remove a collaborator from a test once added.
