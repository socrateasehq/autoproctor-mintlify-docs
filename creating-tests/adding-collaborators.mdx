---
title: "Adding Collaborators to Your Tests"
description: "Share test management access with other team members using AutoProctor's collaborator feature."
---

You can invite other team members to co-manage a test by adding them as collaborators. Collaborators have full access to view results, change settings, and manage the test.

<Note>
Only **Premium** and **Elite** plan users can add collaborators. Collaborators must already be members of your [team](/pricing/teams). If someone is not yet on your team, invite them to join your team before adding them as a collaborator.
</Note>

## How to Add a Collaborator

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Advanced Settings">
    Navigate to your test and open the **Advanced Settings** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the Collaborate Link">
    Click the **collaborate link** to copy it to your clipboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the Link">
    Send the link to the team member you want to add as a collaborator.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collaborator Accepts">
    When the person clicks the link, they gain access to the test immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/creating-tests/collaborators-section.png" alt="Collaborators section in AutoProctor Advanced Settings" />
</Frame>

<Warning>
Collaborator access is **permanent** — there is currently no way to remove a collaborator from a test once added. Collaborators receive full read and edit access, including the ability to change settings and view all results.
</Warning>
